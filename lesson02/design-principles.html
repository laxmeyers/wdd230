Hick's Law

Church of Jesus Christ
www.churchofjesuschrist.org

The Church of Jesus Christ does a good job with Hick's Law. On the first page you see four main choices. These open bigger tabs to select choices from. They help narrow down the selection of what you are looking for. When you aren't sure what you are trying to find the four main tabs help narrow it down. You can guess which tab it will be in. Once inside these tabs it is easy to find what you are looking for. When you search you can expand or narrow down what you are looking at from talks to scriptures.

Visual Hierarchy

Apartment listings Providence Square
www.apartments.com

This page really helps direct your eyes to where they want them to go. The picture is the first thing as that is front and center. There are many examples of the subtitles going with their examples. Helps you follow along as you scroll down. On the right hand side are the similar listings. Those catch your eyes to look over there if you don't like what you see. Helps to show you many more options and catch your attention.

P.A.R.C.: Proximity

BYU Devotional Speeches
speeches.byu.edu/

This is the proximity of objeccts to denote what paragrapghs go to what subtitle or picture. When we look at the speeches byu page we see this in action. The subtitles with the rotating talks with the pictures lets you see what talks the subtitles describe. Within these little boxes the pictures go with the talks as you see a border to contain what all is with that certain talk. This all helps you navigate the page knowing what you are looking for that the helpers like titles show you where you may find them.
