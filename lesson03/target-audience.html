Target Audience

This site will try to be a site for all residents or potential residents. It will aim to help businesses bring in customers while helping customers find those businesses. This will also be a good site to allow people thinking of moving here a site to see what the city can provide. The companies will be able to find people to hire while the employees can see companies they can work for. Everyone should be able to find something helpful off this site.

Personas 1

Farnsworth Mitchell

Buisness Owner of Mitchell Realistate
Farnsworth is wanting a place to find all the workers he needs. It has always been a struggle for him to find good help to hire. He has always had good business with many people coming to him to help sell their homes. He just needs the workers so he doesn't have to do all the work. There needs to be an easy way to find workers with the right key words and to have those people find his business easily.

Goals:

  • Support his family of 5 kids and a wife
  • Grow his business into all of the city
  • Be able to pass down the business

    • Background
    Farnsworth grew up in this town. When it was first really all just farmland. With the expansion of the residential areas in his town he wanted to do realistate. He saw an opportunity to make money and he knew the place better than any other realitors in the area. He went to college for business and came out as an entrepreneur. His wife is his highschool sweetheart and they have been here their whole lives.

    Personas 2

    Abigail Blotwells

    Loooking for a job in another city.
    Abigail was let go recently from her job in Montana. She has been in Montana 23 years and was wanting a change. She isn't married and stills wants to be married. Can't seem to find the right guy. Thought a different state would be a good change to find someone else. She was a live in nanny for a family back in Montana. Looking to find a live in nanny position or any job really. Wants to make connections and further her education.

    Goals:

  • Find a nice man to marry
  • Increase her education
  • figure out what to do with her life

    • Background
    Abigail says she is from Florida, as that is where she was born. Her family moved to Montana when her dad got a job offer as a mechanical engineer. They lived there the whole time since. Abigail likes to sound more interesting and thus why she says she is from Florida. When she graduated from high school she went to colledge for about 3 weeks before dropping out and taking the live in nanny for a couple with 3 younger kids that had moved into her home town in Montana. She regrets leaving college and taking the job but thought this would be best for her. Never figured out what to do with her life.
